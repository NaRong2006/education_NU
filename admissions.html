REGULATION ON ENROLMENT

Admission 

Enrolment of year I (Foundation Studies) shall be made only when students have High School Certificate (GCE) or equivalent certificate/document. 

Graduate students shall have a bachelor's degree or equivalent degree. 

Students shall take the entrance exam or any requirements set by Norton University. 

Students holding degrees or other certificates from other Educational Institutions and wishing to pursue their studies in the second year or third year at Norton University shall go through an evaluation process conduct by the Norton University Academic. Board and duly approve by the Ministry of Education, Youth, and Sports. 

All students shall pay tuition fees to Norton University before the next academic year starts and the fees is nonrefundable.

ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

For Local Students 

1. Original copy of BACC II (G12 National Examination Certificate). 

2. Four (4) copies of 4cm x 6 cm photos. 

3. Complete Application Form and Registration Fee. 

4. Entrance Test 

5. High School Transcript 

For Foreign Students 

1. Original High School Diploma (with English translation, if not written in English language) 

2. Original Alien Certificate of Registration (ACR) issued by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs or passport. 

3. Personal History Statement. 

4. Complete application form and admission fee (USD 15.00) 

5. Six (6) copies 4x6 photos 

6. Transcript of records (with English translation) 

7. A “Passing Mark” in the English language test (in case of poor English language comprehension, the student shall be required to enroll at Norton's Language Institute for special English class) 

8. Medical health certificate with standard size chest X-ray

Graduate School Admissions

General Admission Requirements 

The applicant should: 
1. Hold a Bachelor's degree in a related field of study from an institution recognized by the government of the country in which the institution is located 
2. Be proficient in English 
3. Have at least two years related working experience 
4. Pass the written and oral examinations conducted by the Graduate School 

Application Documents 

The following items must be submitted on the date of application: 
1. A completed application form 
2. An authenticated copy of the Transcript of record 
3.Two copies of 3x4cm photographs with any background 
4.One copy of 4x6cm photograph with any background
